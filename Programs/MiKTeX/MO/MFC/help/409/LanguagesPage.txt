.topic HIDC_LIST
Displays a list of all languages known by TeX.

.topic HIDC_NEW
Click this to create a new language definition.

.topic HIDC_REMOVE
Click this to remove the selected language definitions.

.topic HIDC_EDIT
Click this to display or change the selected language
definition.

.topic HIDC_MOVEUP
Click this to move the selected list entry up.

.topic HIDC_MOVEDOWN
Click this to move the selected list entry down.
